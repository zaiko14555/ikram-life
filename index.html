About Me

Hi! I’m Ikram, 15 years old. I love drawing, listening to The Weeknd & Billie Eilish, and watching anime like JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure. I adore hats 🎩, the calmness of the night 🌌, and also I love playing Roblox 🎮. Even though I face bullying sometimes, I stay positive and express myself through art.

My Story

Since I was a little kid, I always felt different. My love for music and art helped me escape from the noise of the world. When people bullied me, I didn’t give up — instead, I drew my feelings on paper, painted with colors, and listened to The Weeknd and Billie Eilish late at night.

Anime, especially JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure, inspired me to stay strong, creative, and unique. Every character showed me that even in a strange world, being yourself is the real power.

Today, my drawings, my dreams, and my love for Roblox are my answer to all the negativity. I want to shine like the stars in the night sky, and to keep my soul filled with art, music, games, and love.
